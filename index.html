Our Story

Where Flavor
 Meets Passion

Welcome to Steak Bistro! We are a cozy and friendly restaurant in the heart of Cabuyao, Laguna, Philippines. Our chefs are passionate about food and love to mix classic steaks with the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine. Whether you’re here for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special occasion, we promise a great meal and warm hospitality.

A Selection of
Delicious Starters

Fresh Lumpia:

A refreshing Filipino spring roll filled with crisp vegetables, noodles, and savory meat, served with a sweet peanut sauce.

Ensaymada:

A sweet, fluffy Filipino pastry topped with sugar, grated cheese, and butter, offering a deliciously rich, comforting treat.

Sinigang Soup:

A tangy and savory Filipino soup made with tamarind, tender meat, and fresh vegetables, delivering a bold, comforting flavor.
